About Me

I am Prince of Host, a passionate yoga practitioner and Guinness World Record Holder for maintaining the Tree Pose (Vrikshasana) for 4 hours continuously. My journey in yoga began over a decade ago, driven by a quest for balance, holistic wellness, and the joy of teaching others. Through dedication, discipline, and the guidance of inspiring teachers, I have helped hundreds of students discover the transformative power of yoga. My mission is to make yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of age or experience, and to foster a supportive community focused on growth and well-being.
